About Us

At Petra Firma,

Precision. Strength. Innovation. — these are the pillars on which we build.

We specialize in cutting-edge steel detailing and structural engineering solutions, laying the groundwork for resilient and efficient structures.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between architectural vision and structural reality by delivering accurate, reliable, and cost-effective detailing services.

Beyond detailing, Petra Firma extends its expertise into construction execution, offering end-to-end solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From elegant interiors and robust exteriors to specialized greenhouse constructions, we are committed to transforming spaces with precision craftsmanship and timely delivery.

Why Choose Us?

Petra Frames isn’t just a service provider—we’re your long-term structural partners, committed to building the skeletons of tomorrow’s skylines.

Our team comprises seasoned engineers and detailers with decades of combined experience in steel fabrication, connection design, and BIM modeling.

We leverage advanced tools like Tekla Structures, AutoCAD, and Revit to ensure millimeter-perfect detailing and clash-free designs.

From small-scale projects to industrial complexes, we tailor our services to meet your deadlines, budgets, and quality standards.

What We Do
On Structural & Steel Detailing

Structural & Miscelleneous Steel Detailing

Precise detailing for structural and miscellaneous steel components, ensuring accurate fabrication, erection, and seamless integration with engineering standards.

Bridge Detailing

Comprehensive bridge detailing solutions that meet safety standards and structural codes, supporting efficient fabrication and smooth on-site assembly.

Connection Design & Stamping

Engineered connection designs with professional stamping, ensuring structural integrity, code compliance, and clear communication between design and fabrication teams.

Estimation Support Service

Accurate and timely estimation support to streamline bidding, budgeting, and planning for steel projects of all sizes and complexities.

Client Specific Detailing Automation

Customized detailing automation tailored to client standards, reducing manual errors, improving efficiency, and delivering consistent, repeatable results.

Decking Support & Detailing

End-to-end decking support and detailing, including layout, openings, and edge conditions—ready for fast, accurate field installation.

Stick Modelling & Take Offs

Efficient stick modeling and material take-offs to support procurement, fabrication planning, and accurate project cost forecasting.
